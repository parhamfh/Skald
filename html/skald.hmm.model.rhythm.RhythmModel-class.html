Strange distribution ===

Starts out (round 1) by assuming equal distribution amongst the transitions to receive probabilities, calculates probability based on sum of probabilities being 1. After this round it assumes equal distribution between remaining transitions to receive probabilities, and calculates probability from 1-`previous probability`. And goes on till final transition probability remains which is given the remainder of what is left of 1.
